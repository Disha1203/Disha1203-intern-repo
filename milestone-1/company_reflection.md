## Mission

To help people enhance their focus, productivity and wellbeing by minimizing distractions and directing them with healthy habits and routines. Has a strong focus on helping neurodivergent individuals , as they may find traditional productivity tools difficult.

## Vision

To offer a holistic productivity solution by offering distraction blocking, habit coaching, break management and behavior change strategies all on one platform. Focus Bear is not just about tracking habits; it's also about supporting its users to actually implement their actions and create lasting habits that enhance their lives.

## Reflections

### Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

Focus bear was created to help neurodivergent individuals to manage their routine and time well.
* It was created since most apps aren't well suited for neurdivergent individuals 
* Since the founders experenced similar challenges , the team build it to actively guide users through routines and block distractions

### What problem is Focus Bear solving?

It aims to solve issues neurodivergent individuals come across such as 
* executive dysfunction
* hyberfocus (tend to ignore taking breaks , sleep)
* decision fatigue
* maintaining routines 
* distractions 

### Why do you think this mission is important?

It's important since screen time has increased for most since distraction is more accessible than ever.
* Social media, gaming , messaging platforms and endless onlines content constantly compete for attention
* Though these affect everyone, it can be especially difficult or people with ADHD and other neurodivergent conditions.

Thus healthy routines and focus has a great impact on the overall quality of life.
* Helping people manage distractions and build sustainable habits can improve many aspects of their daily lives


### How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?

Focus Bear's work aligns strongly with my interests in technology, productivity, and problem solving.

* I belive that a productivity app should be able to help the user even during their worse times, especially when the smallest tasks seem impossible
* Setting even a simple routine can help individuals to have structure and consistency in their life

## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve? Do you know someone who could benefit from it?

Yes. As someone with ADHD,I can personally relate to several of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to address.

I have an issue with **hyper focus**. Hyperfocus is a great trait to have if I want to do work; sometimes, if I am hyperfocused, I forget to take breaks, get food or take a break from my computer. This can cause me to feel overwhelmed and burned out and ultimately, procrastinate with other tasks that need to be done. **Focus Bear's micro-break routine** was a great help in taking regular breaks but not 100% dependent on the will power or frequent reminders.

When learning about things that I am interested in I tend to go down **rabbit holes**. Though this add up my knowledge base, sometimes not the most efficient use of time, particularly when exams or deadlines are coming up. With this **AI blocking feature**, Focus Bear keeps me on track with my intent and helps to avoid going too off-topic.

One thing that I really like is the **routine system**, which is structured. The app should give clear instructions on what to do next, and not leave it to chance or motivation, since there are many steps to take in order to achieve a larger goal. This will make tasks seem less daunting and will help with consistency.

I have personally dealt with these issues and know how Focus Bear could be of benefit to many other students, professionals and neurodivergent individuals who suffer from distractibility, procrastination, hyperfocus and/or a lack of routine.
