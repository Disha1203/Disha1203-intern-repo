* I want to learn about how a problem, like scalability, maintainability, security, is addressed in real world use with an experienced engineer.

Success for me would mean:
* Contributing meaningful features to a real product

By the end of the internship, I hope to look back and see measurable growth both as a software developer and as an engineering team member.
